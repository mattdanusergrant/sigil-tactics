v0.15 — campaign mode · 9-hero roster · 90-card pool · deck = drafted heroes' suites

Modes

  • Campaign: start with Knight, Ranger, Mage. Defeat each of 6 boss heroes (Paladin, Druid, Scout, Berserker, Warlock, Assassin) to add them to your roster. Progress is saved between sessions.
  • Skirmish: single match using the full 9-hero roster, random AI draft.

Match flow

  • Draft (1-2-2-1 snake): P1 picks 1, P2 picks 2, P1 picks 2, P2 picks 1. Each side ends with 3 heroes. In campaign mode, the player picks only from unlocked heroes and the AI picks from a mission-specific priority list (boss + themed allies).
  • Deploy: alternating face-down placement in reverse-of-draft order (P2 places first to offset P1's first-pick advantage). You see where the opponent placed but not which hero is in each tile — units flip face-up when play begins.
  • Play: standard initiative-order combat.

Rules

  • Win: defeat all 3 enemy heroes.
  • Initiative: each round, units act in Speed order (high → low). A unit can move + attack (or skip either).
  • Facing: attacks from the SIDE deal ×1.5, REAR ×2 (Assassin: ×2 / ×2.5).
  • Terrain: ▲ high ground gives +1 ATK while standing on it.
  • Cards: start with 2 in hand, no max. Draw 1 at the end of every turn (yours or the AI's). Each card is one-shot.
  • Opportunity cost: there is no aether. Playing a card replaces the active unit's normal turn — so the choice is "use this unit, or play this card." Aim to always have a card better than the unit's normal action.
  • Shield (⛨N): absorbs damage before HP. Persists across rounds.
  • ATK buff (+NA): bonus damage that clears at round start.
  • Move buff: extra movement on the buffed unit's next turn only.
  • HP dice: every unit's max HP is a polyhedral die face count (4/6/8/10/12/20).

Card categories

  • Equipment (5 per hero) — one each of Weapon, Armor, Helmet, Boots, Artifact.
  • Actions (5 per hero) — moves, attacks, and effects performed by a hero. Spells are a subtype of action (purple stripe, tag SPL) — mechanically identical to actions, distinguished only by flavor.
  • Your deck: 30 cards built at deploy time from your 3 drafted heroes' suites. Pool across the 9 active heroes is 90 cards total.

Card kinds (effects)

  • dmg — N damage to target enemy (shield absorbs first)
  • dmg-adj — N damage to target enemy + S splash damage to enemies orthogonally adjacent to it
  • heal — restore N HP to target ally (caps at maxHp)
  • heal-all — restore N HP to every ally
  • shield — +N shield on target ally
  • shield-all — +N shield on every ally
  • buff-atk — target ally +N ATK this round
  • buff-atk-all — every ally +N ATK this round
  • charge — target ally +N move this turn

Deck — 30 cards (3 hero suites × 10)
